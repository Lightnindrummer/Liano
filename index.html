Who Is Liano?

, I am Liano. I am a music composer on Youtube. I play the piano (which I absolutely love) and the trumpet.

My first album, "Capture That Feeling," was made to explore the emotions I felt playing music. I had spent hours at night improvising stuff that could move me. I wanted to integrate this sort of magic within something that could be given to others. The heavy use of piano throughout the album stemmed from my love for the piano.

Although I enjoyed the process of making piano songs, I actually got kinda bored. It's hard to work with just one instrument ha. I decided to get into the process of making orchestral pieces. My two orchestrations "tulips" and "Avri" are the beginning of a new era for my music. But even now, I still have that urge to drive emotions and feeling into what I write. Storytelling is also playing a role (there's going to be a lot of that).

The greatest gift for me is to move others with music, just like music has moved me.
